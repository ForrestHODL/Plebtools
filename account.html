📡 Configure Relays

Relays are servers that store and forward Nostr events. Configure relays to enable zapable events and full Nostr functionality.

💡 Popular Public Relays:
  • wss://relay.damus.io - Damus relay (recommended)
  • wss://nos.lol - Nostr relay
  • wss://relay.snort.social - Snort relay
  • wss://relay.nostr.band - Nostr Band relay

You can add multiple relays for redundancy. Just paste one URL at a time.
